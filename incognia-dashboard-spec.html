Context & Goals

The existing Looker Studio dashboard was functionally broken: built against raw engagement counts instead of scores, polluted by null company records, and choking on 3M+ rows of LinkedIn impression data. The goal was a complete rebuild with proper data architecture and a spec-driven approach.

Audience

RoleFrequencyDecision
Sales AEDaily / WeeklyWhich 3 to 5 accounts should I prioritize this week?
Marketing LeadWeeklyWhere is marketing generating traction that sales isn't picking up?
Sales & Mkt LeadershipWeekly / MonthlyAre we closing the coverage gap across the target account universe?
RevOps (Lean Layer)Ad-hocIs the scoring model producing meaningful separation?

Scoring Model

Combined account score ranging from -200 to +200, composed of a Marketing Score (-100 to 100) and a Sales Score (-100 to 100). The critical threshold is 50 points on either axis.

Quadrant Model

Well Covered
Both motions ≥ 50. Monitor and maintain.
Hot Handoff
Marketing ≥ 50, Sales < 50. Sales needs to act.
Needs Air Cover
Sales ≥ 50, Marketing < 50. Increase targeting.
Dark
Both < 50. No meaningful engagement.

Success Criteria

An AE can identify their top accounts in under 2 minutes. Marketing can quantify quadrant movement over any period. Any account can be drilled down to contact roster and engagement timeline within one click. Dashboard loads in under 10 seconds.

Accessibility

All critical text elements pass WCAG AA. Navy on white: 16.75:1 ratio. The dashboard never relies on color alone — every quadrant, status, and trend includes a text label alongside the color indicator. Quadrant pill contrast ranges from 7.1:1 to 8.5:1.
